LEMON CAKE

by:

good taste   

these zesty cupcakes look so gorgeous you wont want to eat them but one your start you wont be able to stop

ingredients

  • 225gself-rising flour
  • 175g golden caster sugar
  • zest 2 lemons
  • 1 tbps poppy seds, toasted
  • 3 egs
  • 100 gr natural yogurt
  • 175 g butter, melted and cooled a little
  • for the icing
  • 225g butter, softened
  • 400 gr icing sugar, sifted
  • juice a lemon
  • few drops yellow food colouring
  • icing flowers or yellow sprinkles,decorate

method

step 1

heat oven to 160C/180C fan/gas 4 line a 12 hole muffin tin with cupcake or muffin cakes. Mix the flour,sugar lemon zest and poppyn seeds together in a large mixing bow!Beat the eggs into the yogurt then tips this into the dry ingredients with the melted butter mix together with a wooden spoon or whisk until lump free, then divide between the case.Bake for 20-22 mins untill a skewer poked in comes out clena-the cakes will be quite pale on top still.Cool for min in the tin, then carefully lift into a wire rack to finish cooling

step 2

to ice, beat the softened butter until really soft in the large bowl, then gradually beat in the icing sugar and lemon juice.Stir in enough food colouring for of pale lemon then spoon the icing into a piping bag with a large star nozzle

step 3

ice one cak at a time, holding the piping bag almost upright with the nozzle about 1 cm form the surface of the cake. Pipe one spiral of icing aroud the edgel, then pause to break the flow before moving the nozzle towards the centre slighty and piping a second smaller spiral that continues untill there are no gaps in the centre. slighty'dot' the nozzle into the icing as you stop squeezing to finish neatly. Repeat to cover all the cakes the top with sugar decorations or scatter with sprinkles.
